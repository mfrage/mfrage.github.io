About Our Chapter

Delta Kappa Epsilon (DKE) is a historic national fraternity founded in 1844 at Yale University. We are focused on building lifelong brotherhood, fostering leadership development, promoting service, and encouraging academic & personal growth for its members.

The Delta Alpha Chapter of DKE was established at Auburn University in 2019. Our chapter embodies the values of friendship, academic excellence, and community service. We are committed to developing men of character who contribute positively to Auburn's community and beyond.
